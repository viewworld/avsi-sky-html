Skilling Youth for Employment in Agri-business (SKY)

Through the Skilling Youth for Employment in Agri-business (SKY) project, AVSI Foundation will create employment opportunities for 4,000 youth which will generate an annual total income of Euro 3.6 million while supporting the initiatives of Government of Uganda to develop a market, social and gender-responsive agribusiness skilling.
